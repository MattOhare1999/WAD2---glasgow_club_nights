Cathouse

> Cathouse is Scotland’s original & best rock club. Established in 1990 by owner Donald MacLeod the Cathouse started life in Brown Street in Glasgow before moving to its current, larger home at 15 Union Street in 1995. Now in our 25th year, we’re not afraid to embrace some genres you might not to expect in a traditional rock club. Times change, tastes change and we like to make sure we play a mix of tunes throughout our 3 rooms.

> Hosting space across The Cathouse and our sister club The Garage we have an impressive arsenal of space and equipment at our disposal. If you’re interested in gigging here then check out our contact page for our venue spec.

> We host a tonne of club nights, covering everything you could possibly want from classic rock, metal and punk right into hip-hop and dance. We cater to everyone and exclude nobody so you’re guaranteed to find a night you’ll love.
