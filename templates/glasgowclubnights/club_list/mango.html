Mango Tropical Bar/Club

> Holiday-happy, kitsch and colourful, Mango Tropical Bar & Restaurant brings a sunny sweetness to Glasgow!

> Brimming with beats from Latin America and the Caribbean, this three level restaurant-bar and club certainly creates a deliciously different night-time music feel showcasing everything from reggae and ska to latin and salsa for that ultimate easy-going feel.

> In keeping with the sultry South American theme, Mango Tropical Bar & Restaurant is designed with a bright, vibey look, particularly its slap-dash style collage of colourful posters and maps, so you'll immediately feel like you've been teleported to a boozy Brazilian barrio.
