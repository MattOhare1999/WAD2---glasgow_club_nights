Below is our journey creating Glasgow Club Nights!

    The first step to our journey was deciding what we were going to do our project on! As we are all border-line alcholics we decided to do it on what we know best... nightclubs!

    Discovering bootstrap has been one of our best discoveries throughout this project. Through using its CSS methods we are able to give a professional look and feel website which is fully responsive

    Hello World! first inherited in 1974, now used in our first live page, we had successfully linked up views and URLS and were on our way to creating GlasgowClubNights
